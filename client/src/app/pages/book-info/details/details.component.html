About the Author

John Ronald Reuel Tolkien was an English writer, academic, and philologist. He is best known as the author of the high fantasy works The Hobbit and The Lord of the Rings. He was appointed a Commander of the Order of the British Empire by Queen Elizabeth II on 28 March 1972.

Tolkien was fascinated with language and mythology, which influenced his fictional Middle-earth. His work has inspired countless adaptations and remains influential in the fantasy genre.
